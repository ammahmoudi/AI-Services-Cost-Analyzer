How to Get the wos-session Cookie
Step-by-Step Guide:
Method 1: Application Tab (Easiest)
  1. Login to fal.ai
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  3. Go to Application tab (or Storage in Firefox)
  4. In the left sidebar, expand Cookies
  5. Click on https://fal.ai
  6. Find wos-session in the list
  7. Double-click the Value column and copy it (starts with Fe26.2*1*...)
  8. Paste it in the form above
Method 2: Network Tab
  1. Login to fal.ai
  2. Press F12 â†’ go to Network tab
  3. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
  4. Click on any request to fal.ai
  5. Scroll down to Request Headers
  6. Find the cookie: header
  7. Look for wos-session=Fe26.2*1*...
  8. Copy everything after wos-session= until the next semicolon
Pro Tip: The wos-session cookie is very long (~2800 characters). Make sure you copy the entire value starting with Fe26.2*1* and ending before the next cookie or semicolon.
Important: Cookies may expire after several days. If you start seeing $0 pricing again, come back here and update your wos-session cookie.
